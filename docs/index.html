Why Resale Flat Prices?

  • In Singapore, HDB flats are home to over 80% of its resident population.
  • This application provides a means to explore the official transaction data,
  • and provide a simple price estimation for a given flat type by towns.
  • Historical data price data available at, https://data.gov.sg/dataset/resale-flat-prices.

The Prediction Model

  • Linear regression model considering flat type, town, floor area and the age of the flat.
  • Example resale flat price estimation: 
source("../resale.R")
x <- data.frame(flat_type=c("4 ROOM"), town=c("TAMPINES"),
                floor_area_sqm=c(sample(70:130, 1)), age_year=c(10))
print(x)

##   flat_type     town floor_area_sqm age_year
## 1    4 ROOM TAMPINES             76       10

predict(ddp.lm0, x)

##        1 
## 454025.4
